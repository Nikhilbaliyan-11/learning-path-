About ABES Engineering College

Founded in 2000, ABES Engineering College is a prestigious institution committed to providing quality education in engineering and technology. Our mission is to nurture skilled professionals who will contribute positively to society. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty, we provide a learning environment that fosters innovation and research.

Our Departments

ABES Engineering College offers a variety of engineering courses in fields such as Computer Science, Electronics and Communication, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Civil Engineering. Each department is equipped with modern labs, research facilities, and dedicated faculty to guide students towards excellence.

Admissions Process

Our admissions process is straightforward and transparent. Prospective students can apply online, and admissions are granted based on academic performance and entrance exams. Join us to embark on a transformative educational journey at ABES Engineering College.
